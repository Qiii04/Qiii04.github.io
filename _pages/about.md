## About Me

I am a second year PhD student in Robotics at Colorado School of Mines, working with Prof. Yangming Shi. My research focuses on quadruped robot navigation in complex and unstructured environments.

My interests include:
- Reinforcement learning
- Robot locomotion
- Robot Navigation
- Embodied AI
