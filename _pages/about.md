## About Me

I am a PhD student in Robotics at Colorado School of Mines, working with Prof. Yangming Shi. My research focuses on quadruped robot navigation in complex and unstructured environments, especially construction sites.

My interests include:
- Reinforcement learning for robot locomotion
- Navigation in unstructured environments
- Human-robot interaction
- Embodied AI
